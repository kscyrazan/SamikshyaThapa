Personal Introduction

Namaste, I’m Samikshya Thapa from Nepal. I am a law student who values working with people from diverse backgrounds, an essential aspect of global policy, diplomacy, national and international law. I am driven by a deep commitment to empowering justice through the intersection of legal advocacy, ethical leadership, and cross-cultural collaboration.As someone who is actively engaged in sports and thrives in fieldwork, I bring energy, teamwork, and hands-on experience to everything I do. My academic and personal journey is rooted in the belief that law is not just a tool for regulation, but a powerful instrument for equity, sustainability, and global cooperation. Whether engaging with issues of children rights, human rights, environmental policy, or digital governance, I aim to contribute grassroots advocacy that uplifts marginalized voices and fosters inclusive progress. With a global mindset and a passion for public service, I am working to shape a more equitable and sustainable world, one legal step at a time.

CORE VALUES

Ambition

To become a globally engaged legal advocate and policy leader, working at the intersection of international law, human rights, and sustainability contributing to inclusive, evidence-based solutions within global institutions, and amplifying the voices of marginalized communities through diplomacy, legal reform, and cross-cultural collaboration.

Resilience

My resilience lies in my unwavering commitment to justice and inclusion from facing systemic obstacles, complex cultures, and academic stress, my strength comes from my passion for equity and justice. Growing up in a developing country and pursuing law with a global vision, I have learned to turn limitations into motivation embracing interdisciplinary integration, navigating different cultures, and approaching new challenges with empathy, purpose, and clear goals.

Passion

My passion lies at the intersection of purpose and presence. I bring energy, intention, and heart into everything I do whether advocating through law or evolving in silence. It’s not about noise; it’s about depth. I move with meaning, fueled by a quiet fire to grow and serve.

“Why” I do what I do?

I’m driven by a deep-rooted belief that every individual deserves equal rights and access to justice no matter where they come from, what they look like, or their circumstances. Growing up in Nepal, I witnessed the barriers many face when it comes to fundamental human rights, and it fueled my passion to make a difference. Human rights and social justice are at the heart of everything I do. For me, law is not just about rules; it’s a dynamic force for transformation. Through national and international cooperation and leadership, I aspire to bridge cultural divides and leverage the power of collaborative partnerships to build more inclusive and lasting global frameworks. Long-term viability and creative solutions in law will always guide my work, as I believe in addressing today's challenges while ensuring that tomorrow's generations inherit a world of equal opportunity.

Future Goals

My future Goal is to become a trusted and bona fide legal advocate who advances fair, evidence-informed policies, protects vulnerable communities, and promotes long-lasting solutions in global justice, sustainability, and cooperation. To specialize in the rights of children and become a legal advocate dedicated to protecting and promoting their welfare especially in contexts of conflict, poverty, and systemic injustice. I aim to work on creating stronger child protection laws, advancing access to education and healthcare, and ensuring that every child, regardless of background, grows up with dignity, safety, and equal opportunity.
